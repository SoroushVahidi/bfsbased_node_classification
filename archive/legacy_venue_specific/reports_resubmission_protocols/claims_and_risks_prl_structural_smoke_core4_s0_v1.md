# Claims and risks for PRL rewrite

## Supportable if results are favorable

- Selective graph correction can be compared against a small but standard graph-baseline set.
- The uncertainty gate and score terms can now be examined with real controlled variants.
- The method remains feature-first and only uses graph information selectively.

## Still avoid

- Universal robustness claims.
- Overinterpreting descriptive gate diagnostics as causal without the ablation table.
